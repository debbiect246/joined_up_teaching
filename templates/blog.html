Ideas on good teaching practice

Teaching well requires lots of hard work, practice and skills and knowledge that change quickly. Here are my thoughts on teaching well. If you have any comments or suggestions please contact me using the contact form. Thank you.

8th September 2021

Creating zones

Good teachers are lifelong learners!


Teachers hope to inspire a love of learning in their students so leading by example is a good way to go about this, so good teachers regularly keep up to date with good teaching practice and the latest research on methods of teaching. They also work on their communication skills regularly, updating their ideas about the best ways to engage and inspire all students to do their best.
